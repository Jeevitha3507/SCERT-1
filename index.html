Properties of Matter — Introduction

PARAGRAPH 1 [P001] · Tamil + English · Audio: pg3_tm.mp3
Everything around us is made of matter. Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space. The properties of matter help us understand how different substances behave in our daily lives.

Matter exists in three states: solid, liquid, and gas. Each state has different physical characteristics.

🔬 Activity 1.1: Collect 5 objects. Classify as solid, liquid, or gas.
